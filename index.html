About

We are a small private corporation. Our founder and director has an MSc in Mathematics/Operations Research and more than 20 years of experience.

We have designed, built, implemented, and supported large-scale business and industry systems models and solutions. Our industry experience includes biology and agriculture, telecommunications, business systems, and health services.

Over 13 years in Canadian healthcare services, we have worked on laboratory and hospital information systems, custom built interfaces and automated solutions for data transfer, manipulation, collection and complex analytical reporting, and integration between multiple cross-platform systems.

We have worked with a broad range of stakeholders including technical analysts, architects, health care providers, vendors, health care administrators and managers.

We are a member of IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) - www.ieee.org and CORS (Canadian Operational Research Society) - www.cors.ca
